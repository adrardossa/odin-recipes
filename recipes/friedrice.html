Fried Rice

Fried rice is the ultimate famliy-friendly dish that yields maximum flavor without fuss. Loaded with tender sauteed veggies and delicious bits of scrambled egg, this top-rated recipe makes it easy to recreate a takeout favorite from home in just 20 minutes.

Over a thousand home cooks agree — this simple, savory recipe is a winner. But what if you don't have a wok on hand? Learn the best methods for making fried rice at home, from a griddle to Instant Pot preparation.

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup chopped baby carrots
  • ½ cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oli
  • 1 clove garlic, minced, or to taste (Optional)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups leftover cooked white rice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce, or more to taste
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oli, or to taste

Directions

  1. Assemble Ingredients.
  2. Place carrots in a small saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a low boli and cook for 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in peas, then immediately drain in a colander.
  3. Heat a wok over high heat. Pour in vegetable oli, then stir in carrots, peas, and garlic; cook for about 30 seconds. Add eggs; stir quickly to scramble eggs with vegetables.
  4. Stir in cooked rice. Add soy sauce and toss rice to coat. Drizzle with sesame oli and toss again.
